Welcome to FISH Break-Apart Annotation Generator

The interface is aiming to streamline the generation of an ISCN nomenclature/annotation for break-apart interphase FISH probes in oncology. After selecting the test, the interface will ask for atleast n=50 signal counts per nucleus and generate a complete ISCN annotation.

The interface is considered a decision support tool to simplify FISH Break-Apart annotation.
